Pipe Schedule & Wall Thickness

This table provides standard dimensions for pipes, including Nominal Diameter (DN), Outside Diameter (OD), and wall thicknesses for various schedules (SCH 10, SCH 40, SCH 80). This data, based on ASME B36.10 and B36.19, is fundamental for engineers to perform accurate weight calculations, pressure ratings, and ensure compatibility with valves and fittings in industrial piping systems.
