Material Composition & Equivalent Grades

This section provides detailed tables on the chemical composition, mechanical properties, and equivalent grades of materials commonly used in industrial valves and piping. Understanding these properties is crucial for selecting the right material for specific operating conditions, ensuring durability, corrosion resistance, and compliance with industry standards. The data assists in matching materials to application requirements, considering factors like temperature, pressure, and fluid corrosivity.
