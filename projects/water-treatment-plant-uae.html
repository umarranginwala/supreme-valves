Desalination Plant - UAE

Butterfly valves and check valves for 50 MGD desalination plant

Project Overview

Supply of large diameter butterfly valves and check valves for a new 50 MGD desalination plant in Dubai. All valves designed for seawater service with enhanced corrosion resistance.

Scope of Supply

  • Butterfly Valves: 80 units (DN300 to DN1200, PN16)
  • Check Valves: 60 units (DN150 to DN800, PN16)
  • Gate Valves: 40 units (DN100 to DN600, PN16)

Materials Used

Nickel Aluminium BronzeSuper Duplex Stainless SteelEPDM Seals
