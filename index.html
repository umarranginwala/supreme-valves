Supreme Valves India: Leading Industrial Valve Exporter, Exporter & Supplier in Ahmedabad, Gujarat

Founded by Zahid Ranginwala, a valve technology expert with over 38 years of experience since 1986, Supreme Valves India is a leading IBR certified valve exporter and ISO certified valve supplier based in Ahmedabad, Gujarat. We specialize in exporting, exporting, and supplying high-quality industrial valves to global markets including the Middle East (UAE, Saudi Arabia, Qatar, Oman, Kuwait), Africa, Southeast Asia, and beyond.

As a trusted valve exporter in Ahmedabad and valve exporter from India, Supreme Valves has built a reputation for excellence in application engineering, complete material traceability, and timely deliveries. Our deep understanding of valve technology, combined with decades of industry expertise, enables us to provide tailored solutions for diverse industrial applications across oil and gas, petrochemical, water treatment, power generation, HVAC, chemical processing, and marine industries. We supply API 600 gate valves, API 602 forged valves, ASME B16.34 compliant valves, and custom valve solutions.

Comprehensive Range of Industrial Valves

As a leading gate valve exporter, globe valve supplier, and ball valve exporter, Supreme Valves India offers an extensive portfolio of industrial valves designed to meet the demanding requirements of modern industries. Our product range includes Rising Stem Gate Valves and Non-Rising Stem Gate Valves for reliable shut-off in water distribution and oil & gas operations, Y-Pattern Globe Valves and Angle Globe Valves for precise flow control in chemical plants and steam systems, and Floating Ball Valves and Trunnion Mounted Ball Valves (1-piece, 2-piece, and 3-piece) known for their durability and quick operation in HVAC and chemical industries.

As a specialized check valve exporter, our portfolio includes Swing Check Valves, Non-Slam Check Valves, Dual Plate Check Valves, Wafer Check Valves, and Lift Check Valves, ensuring one-way flow protection for pumping stations, fire protection systems, and energy applications. Our Butterfly Valves (wafer type, lug type, and triple offset butterfly valves) provide lightweight, cost-effective solutions for large diameter applications in water treatment and food processing industries.

For specialized applications, we supply Knife Edge Gate Valves for slurry and viscous media in mining and wastewater treatment, Needle Valves for precise instrumentation control, and Piston Valves for high-pressure systems. Our range also includes Pressure Reducing Valves, Safety Relief Valves, Blow Down Valves, and Flush Bottom Valves for critical safety and maintenance operations.

Supreme Valves also provides advanced flow control solutions including 3-Way Ball Valves (L-Port and T-Port) for flow diversion and mixing applications, Motorized and Pneumatic Control Valves for process automation, and Actuated Ball Valves with electric, pneumatic, or hydraulic actuators for remote control applications.

Filtration & Auxiliary Equipment

To ensure system integrity and efficiency, we offer comprehensive filtration solutions including Y-Type Strainers for steam and gas lines, Basket Strainers for pump protection and cooling water systems, and Moisture Separators for steam and compressed air applications. Our Thermodynamic Steam Traps optimize energy efficiency in power plants and heating systems, while Sight Glass valves enable visual flow monitoring in chemical processing and food production.

Premium Materials & Advanced Features

As a leading stainless steel valve exporter, carbon steel valve supplier, and duplex steel valve exporter, Supreme Valves India supplies valves manufactured from premium materials including Carbon Steel (ASTM A216 WCB, A105), Stainless Steel (SS304, SS316, SS316L, ASTM A351 CF8M), Alloy Steel, Duplex Steel (F51, F53), Super Duplex Steel (F55), Monel, Hastelloy, Inconel, and exotic alloys, ensuring long-lasting performance even under extreme temperature and pressure conditions. All products are supplied with complete material traceability, mill test certificates (MTCs), and compliance documentation as per international standards including ASME B16.34, API 600, API 602, API 6D, ANSI Class 150-2500, DIN, and BS standards.
