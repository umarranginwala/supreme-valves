Return & Exchange Policy

At Supreme Valves India, we are committed to delivering high-quality industrial valves that meet international standards. This return and exchange policy outlines our commitment to customer satisfaction and product quality assurance.

Return Policy

We accept returns for defective products only. All our industrial valves undergo rigorous quality control before dispatch, and we stand behind the quality of our products.

Eligible for Return

  • Products with manufacturing defects
  • Damaged products received during shipping
  • Products not matching specifications ordered
  • Products failing quality inspection upon receipt

Not Eligible for Return

  • Products damaged due to improper installation
  • Products used beyond recommended specifications
  • Custom-made or specially ordered products (unless defective)
  • Products with broken seals or tampered packaging

Return Window

30 Days from the date of delivery for defective products

Returns must be initiated within 30 days of receiving the product. Please contact our customer service team immediately if you identify any defects or issues with your order.

Exchange Policy

Yes, we accept exchanges for defective products or incorrect items shipped. We will replace the product with the same model or an equivalent alternative based on availability.

Exchange Process:

  1. Contact our customer service team
  2. Provide order details and defect description
  3. Return the defective product in original packaging
  4. Receive replacement product within 7-14 business days

How to Initiate a Return

1

Contact Us

Email us at info@supremevalves.in or call +91 76000 07000 with your order number and issue description.

2

Provide Documentation

Submit photos/videos of the defect, invoice copy, and delivery receipt for verification.

3

Receive Authorization

Our team will review and provide a Return Authorization (RA) number within 2 business days.

4

Ship the Product

Pack the product securely in original packaging with RA number clearly marked and ship to our returns address.

5

Inspection & Resolution

We inspect the returned product within 3-5 business days and process refund or replacement accordingly.

Return Shipping & Fees

Reason Shipping Cost
Manufacturing Defect Free (We cover)
Shipping Damage Free (We cover)
Wrong Item Shipped Free (We cover)
Customer Error (Wrong Order) Customer Pays

Refund Policy

Once we receive and inspect the returned product, we will process your refund within 7-10 business days.

  • Refunds will be issued to the original payment method
  • Bank transfers may take 3-5 additional business days
  • Shipping charges are non-refundable (except for our errors)
  • Partial refunds may apply if product shows signs of use or damage

International Returns

For international orders, the same return policy applies with the following considerations:

  • Customer is responsible for return shipping costs unless product is defective
  • All customs duties and taxes are non-refundable
  • Return shipping must include tracking and insurance
  • Extended processing time may apply for international returns (14-21 days)

Warranty Information

All Supreme Valves products come with a manufacturer's warranty against defects in materials and workmanship:

  • Standard Warranty: 12 months from date of delivery
  • Extended Warranty: Available on select products
  • Coverage: Manufacturing defects, material failures, workmanship issues
