I.C. Ball Valve Single Piece Screw End

Investment Cast Ball Valve Single Piece Design with Screw End connections (BSP/BSPT/NPT). Compact design for steam, water, oil, air, and gas applications.

Key Features

  • Single piece compact design
  • BS 5351 compliant
  • API 598 tested
  • PTFE/GFT seats
  • Female threading (BSP/BSPT/NPT)
