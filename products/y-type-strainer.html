Applications

Steam Systems

Protection of steam traps, control valves, and equipment in high-temperature steam lines. Removes scale, rust, and pipe debris.

Oil & Gas

Upstream and downstream applications for crude oil, natural gas, and refined products. Protects pumps, meters, and instruments.

Water Treatment

Municipal water supply, cooling water systems, and industrial process water. Removes sediment and particulates.

Chemical Processing

Corrosive and aggressive media in chemical plants. Available in exotic materials for harsh environments.

Power Plants

Boiler feed water, condensate return, and auxiliary systems. Critical for turbine and equipment protection.

HVAC Systems

Heating and cooling systems, chilled water lines, and air conditioning equipment protection.

Advantages of Y-Type Strainers

Compact Design

Y-shaped body allows installation in tight spaces with minimal piping modifications.

Easy Maintenance

Blow-down plug allows quick cleaning without system shutdown or strainer removal.

Low Pressure Drop

Streamlined flow path minimizes pressure loss and energy consumption.

Equipment Protection

Protects downstream equipment like pumps, valves, meters, and instruments from damage.

Multiple Mesh Options

Available in various mesh sizes (20 to 200 mesh) for different filtration requirements.

High Temperature

Suitable for high-temperature applications up to 540°C (1000°F) with appropriate materials.
