40" Sandwich Valve CL300

The 40 inch sandwich valve is an essential component for large-scale pipeline intervention services. Designed for Class 300 applications, it provides reliable isolation during hot tapping and line plugging on high-capacity transmission lines. Constructed from premium WCB steel with ASME B16.47 Series A flanges.

Applications

Large Pipeline Intervention Major Hot Tapping Infrastructure Repair
