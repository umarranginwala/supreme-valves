Duplex Strainer

A duplex strainer provides continuous filtration by using two basket chambers connected via a common changeover valve. One basket remains in service while the other is isolated for cleaning, ensuring uninterrupted operation for critical processes.

How It Works

  • Dual baskets allow online cleaning by switching the flow from the clogged basket to the clean one.
  • Changeover valve (three-way/plug or butterfly arrangement) diverts flow without stopping the pump.
  • Differential pressure across the basket indicates fouling; plan cleaning before excessive ΔP increases energy costs.

Typical Applications

  • Pumps protection in water, seawater, glycol, and utilities.
  • Process fluids with suspended solids where uptime is critical.
  • Upstream of control valves, heat exchangers, and meters.

Material Options

  • Carbon Steel (WCB) for general service.
  • SS304/SS316/SS316L for corrosion resistance and hygienic utilities.
  • SS904L or Duplex for aggressive chloride service.
