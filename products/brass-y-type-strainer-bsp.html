Brass Y-Type Strainer BSP

Brass Y-Type Strainer with BSP Female Threads. 45° screen angle with removable SS perforated screen. Suitable for fire protection, water, and industrial piping.

Features & Benefits

Y-Type Design

45° screen angle provides efficient inline filtration with minimal pressure drop and easy maintenance access.

Brass Construction

Manufactured from CW617N brass (EN 12165) for excellent corrosion resistance and durability.

Removable Screen

SS 304 perforated screen is easily removable for cleaning and maintenance without system shutdown.

High Pressure Rating

20 Bar pressure rating suitable for fire protection and industrial water service applications.
