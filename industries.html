Industries We Serve

Our valves are trusted by leading companies in a wide range of industries. We understand the unique challenges of each sector and provide tailored solutions to meet their specific needs.

Oil & Gas

Valves for upstream, midstream, and downstream applications, designed to withstand harsh environments.

Petrochemical

Solutions for handling corrosive and hazardous fluids, ensuring safety and reliability in critical applications.

Power Generation

IBR-certified valves for high-pressure steam, cooling water, and ash handling in power plants.

Water & Wastewater

Durable valves for water treatment plants, distribution networks, and wastewater management.

HVAC

Cost-effective and reliable butterfly and ball valves for commercial and industrial HVAC systems.

Marine

Valves designed to withstand corrosive marine environments, approved by major classification societies.

Food & Pharma

Hygienic diaphragm and ball valves designed for sterile applications in the food and pharmaceutical industries.

Metals & Mining

Robust valves for handling abrasive slurries and high-temperature processes in the metals and mining industry.

Pulp & Paper

Specialized knife gate valves and other solutions for handling pulp, paper stock, and corrosive liquors.
