What is IBR Certification for Valves?

IBR (Indian Boiler Regulations) certification is a mandatory requirement in India for valves and fittings used in boiler applications, steam systems, and pressure vessels. The IBR Act, 1923, enforced by the Directorate of Boilers under the Ministry of Commerce and Industry, ensures that valves meet stringent safety, quality, and performance standards for high-pressure and high-temperature applications.

As a leading IBR certified valve manufacturer in India, Supreme Valves India supplies IBR approved gate valves, globe valves, check valves, safety relief valves, and other boiler mountings that comply with IBR regulations and international standards.
